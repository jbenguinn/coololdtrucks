The Process

How It Works

01
Browse or List

Buyers browse curated listings of genuine 1970–1990 trucks — no junk, no clunkers, no compromises. Sellers submit their rig with photos, history, and asking price.

02
We Vet Every Truck

Our team reviews every listing before it goes live. Buyers get peace of mind. Sellers get credibility. Everyone gets a cleaner deal.

03
Deal. Drive. Done.

We connect buyer and seller, handle the paperwork assist, and collect a small finder's fee — our cut for making the whole thing happen clean and fast.
